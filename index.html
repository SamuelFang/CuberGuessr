How to play



Guess the time of the solve based on the reconstruction! Feel free to play around with the virtual cube or a real cube to help you decide on a good answer.



Credit to https://alg.cubing.net for reconstruction tool, and Stuart Clark/CubeDB for reconstructions. For bugs and questions, please email cubemasterofficial@gmail.com. Hope you enjoy the game!
